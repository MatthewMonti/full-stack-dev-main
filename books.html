1619 Project

Book Brief

The 1619 Project is worth reading because it encourages a more balanced, historically grounded appreciation of America today. By examining how slavery shaped the nation’s institutions and culture, it helps readers understand that no era was perfect—yet each contributed to the country we live in now. The book invites a deeper, more honest engagement with the past so we can value the present with clearer eyes.
